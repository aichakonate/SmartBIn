Clean City, Clean Home.

Hello there, we are inviting you to make our city clean and tidy. Each day the pollution and general waste is increasing especially in cities. If we do not take care of the planet, we will be harming ourselves. Our future generation deserves a cleaner environment and so does every living organisms. That's why we are introducing to you the new smartBins.

smartBins allows you to collect points whenever you make a contibution to clean the environment. Sign up in here and you will recieve a smartcard on your phone. smartBins will allow you to collect point by tapping your phone card. You can then use your collected points in various shops for a discount or even for free goods and services. The map provided will give you the locations of smartBins around Dublin city.
